Jonah and the Whale

God tells Jonah to go to Nineveh and deliver His message. What should Jonah do?

Obey God and go to Nineveh Run away and board a ship

Jonah obeys God and goes to Nineveh. He delivers God's message, and the people repent and turn back to God.

Lesson: Obedience brings blessings.

Jonah runs away and boards a ship. A terrible storm arises, and the sailors ask Jonah what to do.

Throw Jonah into the sea Try to row back to shore

The sailors throw Jonah into the sea, and the storm calms. Jonah is swallowed by a giant fish, and he prays for forgiveness inside the fish.

After three days, the fish spits Jonah out onto dry land, and he goes to Nineveh to deliver God's message.

The sailors try to row back to shore, but the storm worsens. They realize they must follow Jonah's advice and throw him into the sea.
